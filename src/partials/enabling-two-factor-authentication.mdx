## Enabling two-factor authentication

1. Download and install [Google Authenticator](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/1066447?hl=en) on your phone.
1. Log in to Bitrise, and select **Bitrise CI** from the left navigation menu.
1. In the upper right corner, click the profile image to open the dropdown menu.
1. Select the **Account settings** option.

   ![account-settings-page.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-b08c42b9-affd-b40d-3c6e-87410001d2fb.png)
1. Select **Security** on the left.
1. Click on **2FA is enabled** under **Two-factor authentication**.

   ![2fa.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-cf53d41b-4a10-c359-3816-2f847f107db4.png)
1. Open your Google Authenticator and scan the QR-code that appears on your screen.
1. Enter the 6-digit code that was generated.
1. Once you have activated your 2FA and saved your recovery codes, you will receive a confirmation email from **letsconnect@bitrise.io**.
