## Reporting Step issues

If you’re a user of a Step which has critical (security or functionality) issues, please create a ticket in the Step’s Issue Tracker. Every Step declares the preferred way of reporting issues with the `support_url` attribute.

If you don’t get a response from the Step’s maintainer for an extended period (for more than a couple of weeks) please create a GitHub issue in this repository: [https://github.com/bitrise-io/bitrise-steplib](https://github.com/bitrise-io/bitrise-steplib) and we’ll try to resolve the issue, following the Abandoned Step policy. Please be patient and keep in mind that everyone who contributes to this collection does so to help you by providing a Step for you to use!
