## Skipping Bitrise CI

If you manage multi-vendor or multi-platform CI workflows, you might want to skip builds on multiple systems or just on Bitrise.

To skip builds on multiple vendors, use the `skip ci` or `ci skip` keywords.

To skip builds on Bitrise only, use the `skip bitrise` or the `bitrise skip` keywords in your commit messages to prevent Bitrise builds from triggering. With these Bitrise-specific skip keywords, you get finer granularity and avoid unintentionally skipping builds on other parts of your CI pipeline.
