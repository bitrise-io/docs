## Adding <NT>Workspace</NT> groups to a project

Assigning a <NT>workspace</NT> group to a project means that all members of that <NT>workspace</NT> group will have the same role on the project. Roles are different based on product access: both [Bitrise CI](/en/bitrise-platform/projects/roles-and-permissions-for-bitrise-ci) and [Release Management](/en/release-management/configuring-connected-apps/release-management-roles-and-permissions) have their own roles.

**An project consisting of <NT>workspace</NT> groups**

Let’s say the <NT>workspace</NT> called *TestSpace* owns a project called *TestProject*. *TestOrg* has the following groups:

- **<NT>Group</NT> Alpha**: they are <NT>assigned to</NT> *TestProject* with an **Admin** role. Members of this group can assign other groups to the project or add outside contributors, change <NT>project settings</NT>, manage roles and <NT><GlossTerm baseform="workflow">Workflow</GlossTerm></NT>s.
- **<NT>Group</NT> <NT>Beta</NT>**: they are <NT>assigned to</NT> *TestProject* with a **Developer** role. Members of this group can run builds, view build logs and view builds.
- **<NT>Group</NT> Gamma**: they are <NT>assigned to</NT> *TestProject* with a **Tester/QA** role. They can only view builds.

There are two ways to assign <NT>workspace</NT> groups to a <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT><GlossTerm baseform="project">project</GlossTerm></NT>:

- You can assign it from the **<NT>Collaboration</NT>** menu of the **<NT>Workspace settings</NT>** page.
- You can assign it on the **<NT>Project settings</NT>** page of the project.

### Assigning a group from the <NT>Workspace settings</NT> page

1. <NT>Log in</NT> to <NT>Bitrise</NT> and hover over the left navigation bar.
1. Make sure you have the right <NT>workspace</NT> selected in the **<NT>Workspace</NT>** menu.
1. Select **<NT>Settings</NT>**.

   ![workspace-settings.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-b9660c7d-79af-481d-c05a-17356619dd07.png)
1. On the left, select **<NT>Collaboration</NT>** then go to the **<NT>Groups</NT>** tab.
1. Choose the group you wish to assign, and click the options menu (⋮).

   ![2025-08-07-assign-group-from-workspace.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-42c797d6-13f2-d5b3-c183-c48546d78983.png)
1. Select **<NT>View details</NT>** and go to the **Projects** tab.
1. Click **<NT>Manage access</NT>**.
1. Configure product access by enabling one or more toggles and then selecting a role for your project in the dialog. You have the option to grant universal access or to set roles on a product basis:

   - **<NT>Admin access</NT>** allows the contributor to manage all aspects of the project, including both the <NT>Bitrise CI</NT> configuration and the <NT>Release Management</NT> apps. Note that this gives full access to an outside contributor!
   - Select a product to assign the group specific roles that only apply to that product. If the project doesn't have a [CI configuration](/en/bitrise-ci) or a [Release Management app](/en/release-management), the respective option won't be available.

   ![2025-08-07-access-in-rm-dialog.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-5b7fd557-dadc-4d73-eaa5-39119cd3b63f.png)

   :::tip[Role cheatsheets]

   You can check out the role cheatsheets here: .

   - [Roles and permissions for Bitrise CI](/en/bitrise-platform/projects/roles-and-permissions-for-bitrise-ci)
   - [Release Management roles and permissions](/en/release-management/configuring-connected-apps/release-management-roles-and-permissions)

   :::
1. Click **<NT>Save changes</NT>** to finalize changes.

### Assigning a group from the <NT>Project settings</NT> page

1. Open your project on <NT>Bitrise</NT> with a user that has the **Admin** [role on the project](/en/bitrise-platform/projects/roles-and-permissions-for-bitrise-ci).
1. On the main page of the project, click on the **<NT>Project settings</NT>** button.

   ![project-settings-button.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-14defaa4-472c-2d09-84df-145dc3aef4f5.png)
1. On the left, select **<NT>Collaboration</NT>**.
1. Go to the **<NT>Groups</NT>** tab.
1. Click on **<NT>Add group</NT>**.
1. In the dropdown menu, select the group you need then click **<NT>Next</NT>**.

   ![2025-08-07-add-group-to-project.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-4023a0e5-a889-7041-05ab-f31969f8e898.png)
1. Configure product access by enabling one or more toggles and then selecting a role in the dialog. You have the option to grant universal access or to set roles on a product basis:

   - **<NT>Admin access</NT>** allows the group members to manage all aspects of the project, including both the <NT>Bitrise CI</NT> configuration and the <NT>Release Management</NT> apps.
   - Select a product to assign the group specific roles that only apply to that product. If the project doesn't have a [CI configuration](/en/bitrise-ci) or a [Release Management app](/en/release-management), the respective option won't be available.
1. Click **<NT>Confirm access</NT>**.
