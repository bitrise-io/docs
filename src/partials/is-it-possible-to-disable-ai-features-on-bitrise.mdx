## Is it possible to disable AI features on Bitrise?

Yes. Customers may disable AI features at:

- Feature level
- Workspace level

Please [contact our support team](https://support.bitrise.io/en/articles/11689194-how-to-submit-a-ticket-to-bitrise-support) if you want to disable or enable any or all AI assisted features.
