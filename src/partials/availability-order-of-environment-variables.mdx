## Availability order of Environment Variables

Environment Variables (Env Var) are available after the Env Var is processed.

There are Env Vars exposed by the Bitrise CLI. These are available from the start: for example, `BITRISE_SOURCE_DIR` and `BITRISE_TRIGGERED_WORKFLOW_ID`.

All other Env Vars are processed and made available as the build progresses.

The processing order is the following:

1. Env Vars exposed by the Bitrise CLI.
1. [Secrets](/en/bitrise-ci/configure-builds/secrets.html): they are processed before a Workflow starts.
1. One-off Environment Variables specified for the build through our API.
1. App Environment Variables.
1. Workflow Environment Variables: when the processing of the specified Workflow starts, the Env Vars specified for that Workflow are made available.

   If the Workflow has Workflows [chained before or after it](/en/bitrise-ci/workflows-and-pipelines/workflows/managing-workflows.html), the Environment Variables of the chained workflows are processed and made available right before the first Step of the Workflow would run.
1. Step inputs: they are exposed for each Step, right before the Step would start.
1. Step outputs: they are exposed by the specific Step, so those are available for subsequent Steps after the Step finishes.
