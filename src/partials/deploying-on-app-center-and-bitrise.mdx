## App Center Distribute and Bitrise Release Management

The App Center Distribute service allows you to manage app distribution across multiple platforms in one place. Bitrise [Release Management](urn:resource:publication:90740) offers exactly that: a one-stop shop for your release requirements. You can:

- Automate all your releases.
- Release your apps across multiple platforms at the same time.
- Have full granular control over the entire release process without leaving Release Management.

To get started, you just need to [connect an app](/en/release-management/getting-started-with-release-management/connecting-an-app). Once you successfully connected an app, you don't have to go through any of the online stores to manage your releases.

We also offer [a fully featured REST API](/en/release-management/release-management-api) to make the most of Release Management.
