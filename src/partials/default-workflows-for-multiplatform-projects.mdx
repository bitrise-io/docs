## Default Workflows for multiplatform projects

For multiplatform projects - projects that aren't native iOS or Android mobile apps but created with some mobile development framework, such as React Native, Flutter, Ionic or Cordova -, Bitrise creates two default Workflows: `primary` and `deploy`.

The exact Steps included in these default Workflows depend on the project type. However, the main goals of the two Workflows are the same, regardless of project type:

- The `primary` Workflow clones your project's repository, installs and caches your dependencies, and runs any tests configured in the project.
- The `deploy` Workflow, in addition to installing and caching dependencies and running tests, also builds your project: it tries to generate an APK or IPA file.
