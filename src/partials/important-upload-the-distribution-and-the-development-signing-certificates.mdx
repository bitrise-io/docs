:::important[Upload the distribution AND the development <NT>signing certificate</NT>s]

We strongly recommend uploading BOTH the development and distribution <NT>signing certificate</NT>s for your project. If you don't have an uploaded development <NT>signing certificate</NT>, <NT><GlossTerm baseform="step">Step</GlossTerm></NT>s with automatic provisioning options will generate one on the fly every time you start a build. This can eventually lead to reaching the maximum number of certificates, blocking you from starting new builds.

:::
