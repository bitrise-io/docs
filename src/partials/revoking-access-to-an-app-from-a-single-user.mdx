## Revoking access to a project from a single user

1. Open your project on Bitrise with a user that has the **Admin** [role on the project](/en/bitrise-platform/projects/roles-and-permissions-for-bitrise-ci.html).
1. On the main page of the project, click on the **Project settings** button.

   ![project-settings-button.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-14defaa4-472c-2d09-84df-145dc3aef4f5.png)
1. From the menu on the left, select **Collaboration**.
1. Go to the **Contributors** tab.
1. Find the user you would like to remove and click "![remove_png.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-ac29f14a-dd0f-9360-8e51-6bc1c83f724d.png)".

   ![remove-people.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-84805be3-7557-14df-e78e-44edaea87777.png)
1. Click **Remove** in the dialog.
