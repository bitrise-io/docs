:::tip[Onboarding with an AI agent]

AI agents and coding assistants can create the user's <NT>Bitrise</NT> account and connect everything directly from their session. The user just provides an email and a one-time verification code, no website visit required. 

Follow the [Bitrise for AI agents onboarding runbook](/en/bitrise-platform/ai/onboarding-for-agents) instead of this guide.

:::

You can sign up for <NT>Bitrise</NT> either via an email address or by authenticating yourself with your <NT>GitHub</NT>, <NT>Bitbucket</NT> or <NT>GitLab</NT> account. Signing up using a Git provider account brings some practical benefits, like logging in with one click and of course you won’t have to authorize your Git account when adding repositories hosted by these providers again.

<Partial_NOTESigningUpWithEmail />


## Signing up with a Git provider

1. You can sign up for <NT>Bitrise</NT> through the **<NT>Start</NT> for free** button in the upper right corner of [Bitrise](https://www.bitrise.io/). It will take you to the **<NT>Sign up</NT>** page.
1. On the **<NT>Sign up</NT>** page, scroll down to the **Or <NT>sign up</NT> with your git provider** section where you can pick a git provider.
1. Authorize your selected Git provider.

   - If you chose <NT>GitHub</NT>, press the **<NT>Authorize bitrise</NT>-io** button, and then you will be redirected to <NT>Bitrise</NT>.

     ![authorize_github.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-245d62ac-c277-cae8-0f6d-6d60ae23bb70.png)

   - If you chose <NT>Bitbucket</NT>, press the **<NT>Grant access</NT>** button on <NT>Bitbucket</NT>, and then you will be redirected to <NT>Bitrise</NT>.

     ![authorize-bitbucket.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-2b51312c-d04b-29f9-c83a-14dcfa9306d2.png)

   - If you chose <NT>GitLab</NT>, press the **<NT>Authorize</NT>** button, and then you will be redirected to <NT>Bitrise</NT>.

     ![authorize-gitlab.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-78feab6d-8731-8d33-b453-bbf6b14a781c.png)
1. Pick a username and a password. When done, click **<NT>Sign up</NT>**.

   :::important[Strong password]

   Please note that you must provide a **strong password** which fulfills these requirements:

   - It should have at least 8 characters.
   - One upper case character.
   - One lower case character.
   - One number.
   - Cannot contain the same character more than 3 times in a row (aaa).
   - Cannot contain your email or username.

   :::

After that, you are ready to roll. Your first <NT><GlossTerm baseform="Workspace">workspace</GlossTerm></NT> will be created automatically, and you will be redirected to the **<NT>Getting Started</NT>** page, where you can rename your <NT>workspace</NT>, run a demo <NT><GlossTerm baseform="Project">project</GlossTerm></NT>, or start adding new projects!


## Signing up with email

:::note[Don't use the + character]

Please note that if you want to sign up for <NT>Bitrise</NT> with an email address, the address cannot contain the character **+**.

:::

1. You can sign up for <NT>Bitrise</NT> through the **<NT>Start</NT> for free** button in the upper right corner of [Bitrise](https://www.bitrise.io/). It will take you to the **<NT>Sign up</NT>** page.

   ![sign_up.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-d7118397-afd2-fcec-a464-e6537b882068.png)
1. Add your email address, username, and password.

   :::important[Strong password]

   Please note that you must provide a **strong password** which fulfills these requirements:

   - It should have at least 8 characters.
   - One upper case character.
   - One lower case character.
   - One number.
   - Cannot contain the same character more than 3 times in a row (aaa).
   - Cannot contain your email or username.

   :::
1. Check the **I agree to the <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Terms of Service</NT> & Privacy Policy**, and click the **<NT>Sign up</NT>** button.
1. Check your inbox for a confirmation email from <NT>Bitrise</NT> and follow the instructions there. If you haven’t received a confirmation email from us, click the **Resend confirmation email** button.

   ![checkyourinbox.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-06a9ef1f-701a-678b-cc0c-8aad5c078cd2.png)

And that's it! After confirming your account, your first <NT>Workspace</NT> will be created automatically, and you will be redirected to the **<NT>Getting Started</NT>** page. You can rename your <NT>Workspace</NT>, run demo apps, or start adding new apps!

![get_started_bitrise.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-cfb7fa41-a106-26b8-af03-a12058f5639f.png)
