On the **<NT>Profile</NT> settings** page, you can:

- Change your username and associated email address.
- Change your password.
- Update your personal and company information.


## Changing your <NT>Bitrise</NT> username, email, and password

1. <NT>Log in</NT> to <NT>Bitrise</NT>, and select **<NT>Bitrise CI</NT>** from the left navigation menu.
1. In the upper right corner, click the profile image to open the dropdown menu.
1. Select the **<NT>Account settings</NT>** option.

   ![account-settings-page.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-b08c42b9-affd-b40d-3c6e-87410001d2fb.png)
1. To change your username, type in the new username in the **<NT>Username</NT>** field.
1. To change your email, type in a new email address in the **<NT>Email</NT>** field.
1. To change your password, type in a new password in the **<NT>Password</NT>** field, and then type it again in the **<NT>Confirm password</NT>** field.
1. When done, click **<NT>Update</NT>**.


## Adding an avatar to your account

You can add your own personalized avatar to your <NT>Bitrise</NT> account.

1. <NT>Log in</NT> to <NT>Bitrise</NT>, and select **<NT>Bitrise CI</NT>** from the left navigation menu.
1. In the upper right corner, click the profile image to open the dropdown menu.
1. Select the **<NT>Account settings</NT>** option.

   ![account-settings-page.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-b08c42b9-affd-b40d-3c6e-87410001d2fb.png)
1. Find your default avatar on the left, above the account name.
1. Click the image.
1. Drag and drop your new avatar in the **<NT>CHANGE AVATAR</NT>** window, or click **Browse** to choose an image from your computer.


## Unselecting notification preferences

By default <NT>Bitrise</NT> sends newsletters and platform updates to the email address you provide under your **<NT>Profile</NT>**. You can unselect this setting with 3 simple steps:

1. Click **<NT>Profile</NT> settings** under your avatar.
1. Click **<NT>Notification</NT>** on the left menu bar. You can see that Newsletters and Platform updates are both enabled by default.
1. If you wish to rather not get any or one of the two, notifications, untick the relevant checkbox/es.
