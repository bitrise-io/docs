We’re always sad to see you go but if you wish to delete your <NT>Bitrise</NT> account, it’s quite simple.

:::caution[<NT>Workspace</NT> ownership]

If you are the only confirmed owner of a <NT>Workspace</NT>, you must transfer the ownership of the <NT>Workspace</NT> to another account, or delete it before deleting your account.

:::

1. <Partial_GettingToYourProfileSettings />
1. On the bottom of the **<NT>Profile</NT>** page, click the **<NT>Delete account</NT>** button and follow the instructions.
