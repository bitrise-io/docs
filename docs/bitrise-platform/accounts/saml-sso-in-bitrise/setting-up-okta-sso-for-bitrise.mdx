## Adding <NT>Bitrise</NT> to <NT>Okta</NT>

<NT>Bitrise</NT> is not an integrated app in <NT>Okta</NT>. You have to add <NT>Bitrise</NT> manually to <NT>Okta</NT> first, then you can configure SAML SSO on it.

We will be jumping back and forth from the <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> account to <NT>Okta</NT> so make sure both pages are available. In practice this means the <NT>Workspace</NT> owner should be logged into <NT>Bitrise</NT> and the <NT>Okta</NT> admin should be logged into <NT>Okta</NT>.

1. Log into <NT>Okta</NT> and click **Admin**.

   ![okta_2.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-1640f128-3b4d-356a-f7d0-5d40e60fa6b3.png)
1. From the left menu, select **Applications** under the **Applications** section.
1. Click on the **<NT>Create</NT> <NT>App</NT> <NT>Integration</NT>** button.

   ![app_integration_okta.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-aa579855-a0d6-a545-6a4f-24f543dd36ab.png)

   This opens the **<NT>Create</NT> a <NT>new app</NT> integration** window.
1. Select **SAML 2.0** option as the **Sign-in method** and click **<NT>Next</NT>**.

   ![create_new_app_integration.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-a52e93de-ff50-544e-c914-5aa20dbdeadd.png)
1. At the **<NT>General Settings</NT>** step, type <NT>Bitrise</NT> into the **<NT>App name</NT>** field. (Optionally, you can add an app logo if you wish.) Click **<NT>Next</NT>**.

   ![general_settings_okta.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-e0ec0de8-1ae0-a3e9-ea57-fb0fa50b58df.png)
1. Head over to your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> and click the **Single Sign On** tab on the left menu.
1. Click the **<NT>Copy</NT>** button to copy the **Assertion Consumer Service URL (ACS URL)**.
1. Head back to <NT>Okta</NT>’s **<NT>Configure</NT> SAML** page and paste the copied URL from <NT><GlossTerm baseform="step">Step</GlossTerm></NT> 7. to the **<NT>Single sign-on</NT> URL** input field.
1. Type <NT>Bitrise</NT> at the **Audience URI (SP Entity ID)**.

   You can download the <NT>Okta</NT> certificate file now, and paste its content or upload the file itself in the **<NT>SAML SSO provider certificate</NT>** field on your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>’s **<NT>Single Sign-On</NT>** page. Even easier if you leave it for later as you will need to fill out the **Assertion Consumer Service URL (ACS URL)** on <NT>Bitrise</NT> anyway. You will fetch this while configuring <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a SAML app on <NT>Okta</NT>. Do not hit **<NT>Configure SSO</NT>** on the **Single Sing-On** page of <NT>Bitrise</NT> just yet.
1. Set the **<NT>Name</NT> ID format** to **EmailAddress**.

   ![saml_sso_config.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-26903c72-7c1d-66a1-37d7-15be1fbd6379.png)
1. Click **<NT>Next</NT>**.
1. Fill out the **Feedback** section. <NT>Hit</NT> **<NT>Finish</NT>**.
1. In **Applications**, go to the **Sign-on** tab, and click **<NT>Edit</NT>** next to **<NT>Settings</NT>**.
1. Scroll down to the **Credential <NT>Details</NT>** section, and make sure that **Application username format** is set to **<NT>Email</NT>**.

   ![credential_details_2.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-23aa2f50-cde3-e87d-c8bd-0c2b1d512540.png)
1. Click **<NT>Save</NT>**.

Congrats! <NT>Bitrise</NT> has been successfully added to <NT>Okta</NT> as an app.


## Configuring <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a SAML app for <NT>Okta</NT>

1. Click the **Assignments** tab of your <NT>Bitrise</NT> app.

   Here you can assign <NT>Bitrise</NT> to individuals/groups. Make sure you assign <NT>Bitrise</NT> to all <NT>Workspace</NT> members who will access the <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> through SAML.

   ![assignments_okta.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-1c93dfa7-c82d-5694-1717-62b9239fe667.png)
1. Click the **Sign-On** tab of your <NT>Bitrise</NT> app. You will see that SAML setup is not completed yet. Scroll down, and click **View SAML setup instructions**.

   ![saml_sso_config_samlsso2.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-22736f46-339f-3986-05c1-cbbcf7eb57f6.png)

   The **How to <NT>Configure</NT> SAML 2.0 for <NT>Bitrise</NT> application** page is displayed. It summarizes all the information you need to set up the SAML connection between <NT>Bitrise</NT> and <NT>Okta</NT>.

   ![configure-bitrise-okta-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-a01cfa75-834c-9ffe-57bc-0a097a6df676.jpg)
1. Copy the **Identity Provider <NT>Single Sign-On</NT> URL** and paste it in your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>’s **<NT>SSO URL</NT>** field.

   If you haven’t pasted the Certificate’s content or uploaded the file itself into the **<NT>SAML SSO provider certificate</NT>** field of your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> yet, you can do so now.

   ![saml_sso_url.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-bce20b25-c8cc-fed6-c43c-627647f4c5a4.png)
1. Click **<NT>Configure SSO</NT>** on your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>.

That's it! From now on, whenever you access the **<NT>Single Sign-on</NT>** page, you can configure the SAML SSO settings.


## Setting up SCIM provisioning in <NT>Okta</NT>

You can set up SCIM provisioning for <NT>Bitrise</NT> using <NT>Okta</NT>.

:::important[Managing <NT>Workspace</NT> groups]

While SCIM is a great way to manage the access of your users for <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspaces</NT>, you will have to do some initial configuration for your groups in <NT>Bitrise</NT> after finishing setting up the SCIM provisioning. For more information about configuring your groups, check out [Adding Workspace groups to a project](/en/bitrise-platform/projects/managing-user-access-to-a-project#adding-workspace-groups-to-a-project).

:::

We will be jumping back and forth from the <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> account to <NT>Okta</NT>, so make sure both pages are available. In practice, this means the <NT>Workspace</NT> owner should be logged into <NT>Bitrise</NT>, and the <NT>Okta</NT> admin should be logged in to <NT>Okta</NT>.

1. On <NT>Bitrise</NT>, [configure domain control and generate your SCIM credentials](/en/bitrise-platform/accounts/saml-sso-in-bitrise/configuring-scim). You need to have:

   - A verified domain.
   - An SCIM base URL and an SCIM authentication token.

   Make sure you save both of these because you will need them during the process later.
1. Log into <NT>Okta</NT> and click **Admin**.
1. From the left menu, select **Applications** under the **Applications** section.
1. Select your <NT>Bitrise</NT> app.
1. Click the **General** tab of your <NT>Bitrise</NT> app.
1. Click **<NT>Edit</NT>** next to the **<NT>App Settings</NT>** section and select the **SCIM** option using the radio button next to **Provisioning**.

   ![scim_setting.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-61cf9962-6cb6-6c24-74d4-132f9a1c066d.png)
1. Click **<NT>Save</NT>**.

   A new tab called **Provisioning** will appear.
1. Head back to <NT>Okta</NT>, and select the **Provisioning** tab, then click on **<NT>Edit</NT>**.
1. Paste the SCIM base URL you copied from <NT>Bitrise</NT> in the **SCIM connector base URL** field.
1. Add **userName** in the **Unique identifier field for users** field.

   ![scim_connection_2.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-96dabdf1-1006-5bdc-4457-92dfb91b8c70.png)
1. Select which provisioning actions you would like to enable using the checkboxes next to **<NT>Supported</NT> provisioning actions**.

   :::important[<NT>Push Groups</NT> are mandatory]

   Please note that you must enable <NT>Push Groups</NT>. This is because on <NT>Bitrise</NT>, <NT>Workspace</NT> membership works via groups and <NT>Okta</NT>/SCIM can control our <NT>Workspace</NT> groups via <NT>Push Groups</NT>.

   :::
1. Select **HTTP Header** from the **<NT>Authorization</NT> Mode** dropdown menu.
1. Copy and paste your SCIM authentication token in the **<NT>Authorization</NT>** field.
1. Click **Test Connector Configuration** to test if everything is working correctly.

   ![test_connector.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-0d2d2b23-f9a9-d57d-3b14-44f085bfcc5f.png)
1. Close the **Test Connector Configuration** window and click **<NT>Save</NT>**.
1. Click the **Provisioning** tab of your <NT>Bitrise</NT> app, then click on **<NT>Edit</NT>** next to the **Provisioning to App** section.

   ![scim_provisioning.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-87a6ec99-9380-826b-9176-13034fefa22a.png)
1. Select which provisioning options you would like to enable and click **<NT>Save</NT>**.

   :::note[Sync password]

   We recommend keeping the **<NT>Sync</NT> <NT>Password</NT>** setting disabled.

   :::

That's it! You can now use SCIM provisioning in <NT>Okta</NT> to manage the access of your users to <NT>Bitrise</NT>.


## Pushing <NT>Okta</NT> groups to <NT>Bitrise</NT> using SCIM

You can <NT>push groups</NT> from <NT>Okta</NT> to <NT>Bitrise</NT> using SCIM provisioning. Pushing a group either links an <NT>Okta</NT> group to an existing <NT>Bitrise</NT> group or creates a new group in your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>.

Either way, by pushing a group, you set up synchronization between an <NT>Okta</NT> group and a <NT>Bitrise</NT> group.

1. Log into <NT>Okta</NT> and click **Admin**.
1. From the left menu, select **Applications** under the **Applications** section.
1. Select your <NT>Bitrise</NT> app.
1. Click the **<NT>Push Groups</NT>** tab.
1. Click on **<NT>Push Groups</NT>** and choose a filtering method.

   ![push_groups.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-da9ac0ed-a8fe-0d52-3e66-a8195414d1fa.png)
1. Find the group(s) you want to push.
1. You must either create a new group, which will create a brand new group in your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> or link the <NT>Okta</NT> group to an existing <NT>Bitrise</NT> group.

   You can choose between these methods using the dropdown menu under the **Match result & push action**.

   :::caution[Linking a group]

   If you decide to link an <NT>Okta</NT> group to an existing <NT>Bitrise</NT> group, keep the following in mind:

   - If the <NT>Okta</NT> and <NT>Bitrise</NT> group names are different, the <NT>Bitrise</NT> group will be automatically renamed to match the name of the <NT>Okta</NT> group.
   - When you manually push a group connected to an existing <NT>Bitrise</NT> group, if the group had members who were only added on <NT>Bitrise</NT> and not included in the <NT>Okta</NT> group, then they will be removed as <NT>Okta</NT> becomes the single source of truth.

   :::

   ![linkGroup.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-e94688f6-c068-8c29-201a-bba3c7373dca.png)
1. Click **<NT>Save</NT>**.

   When the **<NT>Push</NT> <NT>Status</NT>** changes to **<NT>Active</NT>**, the synchronization between <NT>Okta</NT> and <NT>Bitrise</NT> is ready. Your group should be updated in a couple of seconds on your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> as well.

   ![push_status_active.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-28f245c4-7eb3-89b2-1e4d-18d922bbc0a5.png)


## Synchronizing groups and users between <NT>Okta</NT> and <NT>Bitrise</NT>

After [pushing Okta groups to Bitrise](/en/bitrise-platform/accounts/saml-sso-in-bitrise/setting-up-okta-sso-for-bitrise#pushing-okta-groups-to-bitrise-using-scim), you can synchronize your groups and users between <NT>Okta</NT> and <NT>Bitrise</NT>. To do so, you must manage the app integration assignments in <NT>Okta</NT>:

1. Log into <NT>Okta</NT> and click **Admin**.
1. From the left menu, select **Applications** under the **Applications** section.
1. Select your <NT>Bitrise</NT> app.
1. Make sure that **<NT>Create</NT> <NT>Users</NT>** and **<NT>Deactivate</NT> <NT>Users</NT>** options are enabled on the **Provisioning** tab.

   ![provisioning_settings.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-d8a157ed-518f-ad9c-67bb-e66a50263ab7.png)

   :::note[Deprovisioning users]

   If you deprovision a user with SCIM, the user will be automatically logged off <NT>Bitrise</NT> and their PATs will be disabled.

   :::
1. Go to the **Assignments** tab.
1. Click **Assign** and choose **<NT>Assign to</NT> <NT>People</NT>** to synchronize individual users or **<NT>Assign to</NT> <NT>Groups</NT>** to synchronize groups.

   ![assign_people.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-d6dbb0ec-3447-10ab-ba08-1bf5731153be.png)
1. Select the users or groups that you would like to synchronize, then click **<NT>Done</NT>**.

   :::important[Domain verification]

   New users without verified domains will receive an email invitation to join the <NT>Workspace</NT>.

   Existing users will also receive a verification email unless you verify your company domain: [Configuring SCIM](/en/bitrise-platform/accounts/saml-sso-in-bitrise/configuring-scim).

   :::
1. After synchronizing your groups and users, you can check the **<NT>Push Groups</NT>** tab to see if the synchronization is finished.

   You can also manually push your updates by clicking on the dropdown menu in the **<NT>Push</NT> <NT>Status</NT>** column and selecting **<NT>Push</NT> now**.

   ![push_now.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-71166a22-3341-0ee5-6599-6b190f83e717.png)

   :::caution["Push now" overrides the <NT>Bitrise</NT> group]

   Users not included in your <NT>Okta</NT> group will be automatically removed from the linked <NT>Bitrise</NT> group.

   :::
