This guide provides step-by-step instructions on setting up <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a SAML application on [OneLogin](https://www.onelogin.com/).

<Partial_CAUTIONSAMLSSORestrictions />

Before connecting SAML SSO to your <NT><GlossTerm baseform="workspace">Workspace</GlossTerm></NT>:

- Make sure the administrator to <NT>OneLogin</NT> is at hand while setting up SAML SSO connection on <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
- <Partial_SAMLSSOBeforeYouStart />

To configure <NT>Bitrise</NT> on <NT>OneLogin</NT>:

1. Log into [OneLogin](https://www.onelogin.com/) as an Administrator.
1. Click **Administration** on the top bar.

   ![Setting_up_OneLogin_SSO_for_Bitrise.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-e05bd9fd-2b96-f6dd-9d2b-b3ad8ba5510e.png)
1. Select **Applications** and click **<NT>Add</NT> <NT>App</NT>**. This will take you to the **Find Applications** page.

   ![Setting_up_OneLogin_SSO_for_Bitrise.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-90d0f8f9-8de5-2c9c-f8e7-2279dc5d48ef.png)
1. Type `Bitrise` in the search bar and select the **SAML2.0** type from the search results.

   ![Setting_up_OneLogin_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-ba3b2ff2-116c-1ae2-0e8d-72c0e73826ee.jpg)
1. Click the **<NT>Configuration</NT>** tab on the left sidebar. You can change the icon and add descriptions if you wish. Make sure **<NT>Organization</NT> (<NT>Bitrise</NT>)** is ticked. <NT>Hit</NT> **<NT>Save</NT>**.

   ![Setting_up_OneLogin_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-97037eee-6e35-4a1f-1e4e-a252c78b2317.jpg)
1. Click the **<NT>Configuration</NT>** tab again and paste the **Assertion Consumer Service URL (ACS URL)** from your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>’s **<NT>Single Sign-On</NT>** tab to the **<NT>Single Sign-On</NT> URL** field on <NT>OneLogin</NT>. Hit **<NT>Save</NT>** in **<NT>OneLogin</NT>**.

   ![Setting_up_OneLogin_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-74f65dc6-6662-db11-e97a-9ba3d039cea9.jpg)
1. Select **SSO** on the left sidebar.
1. Click the **<NT>View details</NT>** <NT>for the</NT> X.509 Certificate. Copy the content of the **X.509 Certificate** and paste it to the **<NT>SAML SSO provider certificate</NT>** field on the **<NT>Enable Single Sign-On</NT>** page of <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
1. <NT>Go back</NT> to the **SSO** page on <NT>OneLogin</NT> and copy the **SAML 2.0 Endpoint (HTTP)** link. Paste it in the **<NT>SAML SSO</NT> provider <NT>Single Sign-On</NT> URL (SSO URL)** field on <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
1. <NT>Go back</NT> to <NT>Bitrise</NT> and click **<NT>Configure SSO</NT>**.

   ![enablesinglesignonpage.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-11509388-3538-06e7-23f3-7ef27a956386.png)

:::important[Users on <NT>OneLogin</NT> and <NT>Bitrise</NT>]

Make sure the users in your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> are all added to the **<NT>Users</NT>** page on <NT>OneLogin</NT>. If not, go to **<NT>Users</NT>**, then **<NT>New</NT> User** and add the new user. Don’t forget to send out an invite (**<NT>More actions</NT>** drop-down menu, **<NT>Send Invitation</NT>**) to a new user so that the user can activate their account on <NT>OneLogin</NT>. Users must be added to the newly created <NT>Bitrise</NT> app by clicking **Applications** under **User Info** and clicking the **+** sign. Make sure the added users are all <NT>Workspace</NT> members on <NT>Bitrise</NT>.

![application-onelogin.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-f6505a6f-5c4e-7cf1-8a81-f8325898b1be.jpg)

:::

If you’ve completed setting up <NT>Bitrise</NT> on <NT>OneLogin</NT> and connected it with your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>, then all <NT>Workspace</NT> members will get an email from <NT>Bitrise</NT> which contains a link to activate their SSO connection to the <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>.
