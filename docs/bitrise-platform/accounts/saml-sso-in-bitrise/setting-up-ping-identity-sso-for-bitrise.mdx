This guide provides step-by-step instructions on setting up <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a SAML SSO application in [Ping Identity](https://www.pingidentity.com/en.html).

<Partial_CAUTIONSAMLSSORestrictions />

Before connecting SAML SSO to your <NT><GlossTerm baseform="workspace">Workspace</GlossTerm></NT>:

- A PingOne administrator who is logged into [PingOne](https://admin.pingone.com/web-portal/login).
- <Partial_SAMLSSOBeforeYouStart />

To add <NT>Bitrise</NT> to <NT>Ping Identity</NT>:

1. Click **APPLICATIONS** on the top bar of [PingOne](https://admin.pingone.com/web-portal/login).
1. On **My Applications** tab, make sure **SAML** is selected. Click the **<NT>Add</NT> Application** drop-down and select **<NT>New</NT> SAML Application**.

   ![Setting_up_Ping_Identity_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-ba20e7c6-49cf-1ec0-ca6a-5630eb89c357.jpg)
1. At **Application <NT>Details</NT>,** fill out the required fields and click **Continue to <NT>Next</NT> <NT><GlossTerm baseform="step">Step</GlossTerm></NT>**.

   ![Setting_up_Ping_Identity_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-6ebb926e-6275-81b7-fdb8-559128c87944.jpg)
1. At **Application <NT>Configuration</NT>**, leave the **I have the SAML configuration** selected. Fill out the following:

   - **Assertion Consumer Service (ACS)** field: Insert the **Assertion Consumer Service URL (ACS URL)** link here from your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>’s **<NT>Single Sign-On</NT>** tab.

     ![enablesinglesignonpage.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-11509388-3538-06e7-23f3-7ef27a956386.png)
   - **Entity ID field**: Type `Bitrise`.
   - **Signing**: Click the **Sign <NT>Response</NT>** option.

     Here is an overview of the above settings: ![Setting_up_Ping_Identity_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-b3f12826-15e7-22c6-5688-9f3be3f519fd.jpg)
1. Continue to the next step.
1. At **SSO Attribute Mapping**, you don’t have to change anything, proceed to the next step.
1. At **<NT>Group</NT> Access**, add the group(s) to your application. These groups will be able to access <NT>Bitrise</NT> through SAML SSO. Continue to the next step.

   ![Setting_up_Ping_Identity_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-cfafb8d7-1a0e-d967-5609-385cc5db90e2.jpg)
1. At the **<NT>Review</NT> <NT>Setup</NT>** page, you can doublecheck the details you provided in the previous steps. Make sure you download the following files from this review page:

   - Click **<NT>Download</NT>** to get the **<NT>Signing Certificate</NT>** and **SAML Metadata** files.

     ![Setting_up_Ping_Identity_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-d626ceb8-0cf3-d459-5cd7-9e59e0fef81a.jpg)
1. Click **<NT>Finish</NT>**.
1. Let’s open the two files.

   - Copy the entire content of the **SAML Metadata** file and paste it in the **<NT>SAML SSO</NT> provider** **certificate** field of your <NT>Bitrise</NT> **<NT>Single Sign-On</NT>** page.
   - Open the **<NT>Signing Certificate</NT>** file and copy the `HTTP-POST` `SingleSignOnService Location` link and paste it in the **<NT>SAML SSO</NT> provider <NT>Single Sign-On</NT> URL (SSO URL)** field on <NT>Bitrise</NT>.

     ![enablesinglesignonpage.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-11509388-3538-06e7-23f3-7ef27a956386.png)
1. Click **<NT>Configure SSO</NT>** on <NT>Bitrise</NT>.

Now you have set up SAML SSO on your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>.
