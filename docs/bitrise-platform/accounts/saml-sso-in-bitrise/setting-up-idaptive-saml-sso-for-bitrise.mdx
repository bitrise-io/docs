This guide provides step-by-step instructions on setting up <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a SAML application on [Idaptive](https://docs.cyberark.com/remote-access-standard/latest/en/content/admin/settings-idaptive.htm).

<Partial_CAUTIONSAMLSSORestrictions />

Before connecting SAML SSO to your <NT><GlossTerm baseform="workspace">Workspace</GlossTerm></NT>:

- <Partial_SAMLSSOBeforeYouStart />
- You must be logged into your Admin Portal on [Idaptive](https://docs.cyberark.com/remote-access-standard/latest/en/content/admin/settings-idaptive.htm) to set up <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a SAML SSO app and establish the connection between <NT>Bitrise</NT> and <NT>Idaptive</NT>. If you are using the **User Portal**, **<NT>Switch</NT> to** **Admin Portal** by clicking your avatar on <NT>Idaptive</NT>.

  ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-27ceb4dd-22a3-a162-b569-73261ba2fef7.jpg)

1. Log into <NT>Idaptive</NT> as an Admin.
1. Go to **<NT>Apps</NT>,** then to **Web <NT>Apps</NT>**. Click the **<NT>Add</NT> Web <NT>Apps</NT>** button on the right.

   ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-9d5b8607-e8f3-2fa7-aa78-8891c184533e.png)
1. On the **<NT>Custom</NT>** tab and select **SAML**, and click **<NT>Add</NT>**. On the **<NT>Add</NT> Web <NT>App</NT>** popup hit **Yes**. **<NT>Close</NT>** the window. You will be automatically directed to the **<NT>Settings</NT>** page.

   ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-7389987f-3a26-24d3-6298-787eb51002e0.png)
1. Add <NT>Bitrise</NT> to the **<NT>Name</NT>** and **Application ID** fields on the **<NT>Settings</NT>** page and click **<NT>Save</NT>**.

   ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-81638e95-5198-7da7-ecb8-f2d9835f64be.jpg)

   ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-175bfd17-e0a5-d198-a2f9-36dc24735c28.jpg)
1. Click **Trust** on the left menu bar and select the **Manual configuration** under **Identity Provider Configuration**.
1. Click the **<NT>Signing Certificate</NT>** dropdown and download the certificate. Open it with a text editor so that you can copy the full content of the certificate. You can also upload the file.

   ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-904a51d4-081b-8a05-0b56-3522007e3a46.jpg)
1. Insert the content or upload the file itself in the **<NT>SAML SSO provider certificate</NT>** text box on the **<NT>Enable Single Sign-On</NT>** page of <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
1. Copy the **Single Sign On URL** from the **Trust** page of <NT>Idaptive</NT>. Insert it on the **<NT>SAML SSO</NT> provider Single Sing-On URL (SSO URL)** field on the **<NT>Enable Single Sign-On</NT>** page of <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
1. While on the **<NT>Single Sign-On</NT> page** of <NT>Bitrise</NT>, copy the **Assertion Consumer Service URL (ACS URL)** URL and click **<NT>Configure SSO</NT>**.
1. Now let’s head back to <NT>Idaptive</NT>! Under **<NT>Service</NT> Provider Configuration** click **Manual <NT>Configuration</NT>**. <NT>Type</NT> **<NT>Bitrise</NT>** in the **SP Entity ID / Issuer / Audience** and paste the **Assertion Consumer Service URL (ACS URL)** from <NT>Bitrise</NT> to the **Assertion Consumer Service (ACS) URL** on <NT>Idaptive</NT>.

   ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-97ca8e78-eae6-ff2b-f6b5-523718161a8e.jpg)
1. Scroll down to **NameID <NT>Format</NT>** and select **emailAddress**. Click **<NT>Save</NT>**.

   ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-5b529235-9f88-8b16-64c4-9438f3cc9498.jpg)
1. Go to **Permissions** and click the **<NT>Add</NT>** button. In the **<NT>Select</NT> User, <NT>Group</NT>, or Role** popup, type the user name you want to add to the SAML app. Select it and hit **<NT>Add</NT>**. Save your changes. This will change the status of your <NT>Bitrise</NT> SAML app to **Deployed**.

   ![Setting_up_Idaptive_SAML_SSO_for_Bitrise.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-fb6f7300-1ac4-523d-0a04-b6c2850e5621.png)

You are now ready to use <NT>Idaptive</NT> as your SAML SSO provider on <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
