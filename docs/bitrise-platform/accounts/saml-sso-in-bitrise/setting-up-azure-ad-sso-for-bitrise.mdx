## Adding <NT>Bitrise</NT> to <NT>Entra ID</NT> as a new application

1. Log into [Microsoft Azure](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/) as an admin.
1. Click the **Azure <NT>Active</NT> Directory** icon on the **Azure services** page.

   ![azureactivedirstep2-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-bf8ba810-c8e2-5323-8d6b-30b9a030b59d.jpg)
1. Click **<NT>Enterprise</NT> applications** under **<NT>Manage</NT>**.

   ![enterpriseapps-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-39f030dd-13a5-7302-6200-44aa39114f17.jpg)
1. Click **<NT>New</NT> Application** to add <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a new app to your account.

   ![newapplication-jpg.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-fd697193-d536-bade-12f6-341e9ecb2df9.jpg)
1. Type `Bitrise` in the **What’s the name of your app?** field. The **Integrate any other application you don’t find in the gallery** button should be automatically selected. Hit **<NT>Create</NT>**.

   ![createyourownapp-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-4822cd7c-0379-35e2-9ab3-d483dc8c6355.jpg)

   You will find your newly created app listed on the **All Applications** page.
1. Click the **<NT>Bitrise</NT>** app to go to its **<NT>Overview</NT>** page.
1. Continue with configuring <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a SAML app.


## Adding users/ groups to the app on <NT>Entra ID</NT>

Before setting up SAML to the app, you have to add all the users/groups to the app in <NT>Entra ID</NT> who will use SAML SSO to log into the <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>. In other words, every <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> member must be added as user in <NT>Entra ID</NT>.

1. Select **<NT>Users</NT> and groups** from the left menu.
1. Click **+ <NT>Add</NT> user/group**.

   ![azureuser1-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-f90189a9-c8f8-0720-69f3-b02983eb74f3.jpg)
1. On the <NT>Users</NT> page of **<NT>Add</NT> Assignment**, select users from the list and click **<NT>Select</NT>**. Once it’s done, you can select a role for users under the **<NT>Select a role</NT>** dropdown.

   ![azureuser2-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-ea5d2cad-6d99-e1e7-e389-6cb1c2170256.jpg)
1. On the **<NT>Add</NT> Assignment** page, click **Assign** to finish adding users.

   ![azureuser3b-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-ee6c9c9d-82a3-5470-9e0d-f5dd4f971414.jpg)


## Setting up SAML SSO between <NT>Bitrise</NT> and <NT>Entra ID</NT>

1. Click **<NT>Single sign-on</NT>** on the left menu. Select **SAML**.

   ![singlesignonazuread.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-9a20bdb0-2c68-3ce5-1f41-ac710e25b7be.png)
1. You will land on the **<NT>Set up</NT> <NT>Single Sign-On</NT> with SAML** page.

   ![sso2-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-6b74d41d-4d91-adb5-0d73-17380c293ab9.jpg)
1. Click the pencil symbol at **<NT>Basic SAML Configuration</NT>** to edit two fields.

   ![sso2a-1.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-954473da-632b-0628-2a76-9ff4471fcc9d.jpg)
1. Add `Bitrise` as the **Identifier (Entity ID)**. Leave this window open! We will come back to it with some information from <NT>Bitrise</NT> in a second.

   :::important[Multiple <NT>workspaces</NT> with the same Entra instance]

   If more than one <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>workspace</NT> uses the same EntraID instance, each <NT>workspace</NT> needs to be configured with its own distinct Entity ID. For each <NT>workspace</NT>, use its own Entity ID at any subsequent step in the process that requires it.

   For example, if you have an Alpha <NT>workspace</NT> and a Beta <NT>workspace</NT>, you can set **alpha** and **beta** as the Entity IDs.

   :::

   ![sso3-2.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-39c02a1a-b3f2-3922-a9d1-fac312c98f92.jpg)
1. Head back to your <NT>workspace</NT> on <NT>Bitrise</NT>: open the **<NT>Workspace settings</NT>** page and go to **<NT>Single Sign-On</NT>**
1. If you have more than one <NT>workspace</NT> using the same Entra instance, add its own Entity ID in the **<NT>Application identifier</NT>** field.
1. Click the **<NT>Copy</NT> Link** button to copy the **Assertion Consumer Service URL (ACS URL)** from <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
1. Let’s head back to the **<NT>Basic SAML Configuration</NT>** window of <NT>Entra ID</NT>.
1. Paste the **Assertion Consumer Service URL** from <NT>Bitrise</NT> to the **Reply URL field on the <NT>Basic SAML Configuration</NT>** page of <NT>Entra ID</NT>.
1. Click **<NT>Save</NT>** and close the **<NT>Basic SAML Configuration</NT>** window.

   ![616541a82bf14.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-681bee8c-6be9-b47f-06fa-72a587e7add7.jpg)
1. On the **<NT>Single sign-on</NT>** page of <NT>Entra ID</NT>, scroll down to the **<NT>Set up</NT> <NT>Bitrise</NT>** section.
1. Copy the **Login URL** and paste it to the **<NT>SAML SSO</NT> provider <NT>Single Sign-On</NT> URL** **(<NT>SSO URL</NT>)** field on <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
1. On the **<NT>Single sign-on</NT>** page of <NT>Entra ID</NT>, scroll up a bit to the **SAML <NT>Signing Certificate</NT>** section.
1. Click **<NT>Download</NT>** next to **Certificate (Base64)** to download the certificate to your local computer.

   ![singlesignonsummary.jpg](/img/_paligo/uuid-da7593a8-e6df-1d97-e903-d375dc504b05.jpg)
1. Open the certificate file and copy/paste its content into the **<NT>SAML SSO provider certificate</NT>** field of <NT>Bitrise</NT> or you can upload the file itself from your local computer too.

   (If manually adding the content, you will need the full content (including `----BEGIN CERTIFICATE-----` and `-----END CERTIFICATE-----` as well).
1. Open the **<NT>Workspace settings</NT>** page on <NT>Bitrise</NT> and go to **<NT>Single Sign-On</NT>**.
1. Click **<NT>Apply</NT> SSO configuration**.

You have successfully set up <NT>Bitrise</NT> as a SAML SSO app on <NT>Entra ID</NT>.
