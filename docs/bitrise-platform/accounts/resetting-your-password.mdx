You can log into <NT>Bitrise</NT> in many ways:

- with your <NT>Bitrise</NT> username and password
- with your <NT>Gitlab</NT>/<NT>Github</NT>/<NT>Bitbucket</NT> account
- with your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT> name if SAML SSO has been set up to and enforced on your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>Workspace</NT>

If you try to log in but you forgot your password:

1. Click **Forgot your password** on our [Login page](https://app.bitrise.io/users/sign_in).
1. Provide your **<NT>Email</NT>** or **<NT>Username</NT>** so that we know where to send password recovery link.
1. Check your inbox for the **<NT>Reset password instructions</NT>** sent by letsconnect.
1. Click **Reset <NT>Password</NT>** or copy the URL into your browser.
1. Add your new password and hit **<NT>Save</NT>**.
1. Once your password has been changed, click **<NT>Log in</NT>** to access our login page again.
1. Add your **<NT>Username</NT>** and new **<NT>Password</NT>** to log into your <NT>Bitrise Dashboard</NT>.

If you failed to log in for the third time, [reCAPTCHA](https://developers.google.com/recaptcha/) will get enabled to check if you are a human.

If you failed to log in for an excessive amount of attempts, you will automatically get locked out. Check your inbox for our **<NT>Unlock Instructions</NT>** email!
