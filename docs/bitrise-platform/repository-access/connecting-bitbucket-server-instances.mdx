Connect your <NT>Bitrise</NT> <NT>workspace</NT> to <NT>Bitbucket Server</NT> to access your privately hosted repositories for CI builds. You need to:

- Create a new Application Link on your server.
- <NT>Authorize Bitrise</NT> and assign the required permissions.
- Copy the necessary credentials to your <NT>workspace settings</NT>.

1. <NT>Log in</NT> to your <NT>Bitbucket Server</NT> instance with **admin** privileges.
1. Click the settings icon on the top menu bar to access **Administration**.
1. On the left menu bar, find the <NT>Integrations</NT> section, and select **Application Links**.
1. Click **<NT>Create</NT> link**.

   ![2025-11-13-bitbucket-app-links.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-f87af39b-bb44-1167-0051-389e3b38c612.png)
1. Select **External Application**, and **<NT>Incoming</NT>** as the direction.
1. In the <NT>Name</NT> field, enter a name that clearly identifies the application. For the sake of simplicity, we recommend **<NT>Bitrise</NT>**.
1. In the **Redirect URL** field, enter https://app.bitrise.io/users/auth/bitbucket_server/callback.
1. Under **Application permissions**, find **Repositories** and select **Admin**.
1. Click **<NT>Save</NT>**. You will be taken to the **<NT>Credentials</NT>** page.

   ![20251113-bitbucket-credentials.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-dbf18dae-38c9-f32a-fb61-6afbb08edb2f.png)
1. Copy the **<NT>Client ID</NT>** and the **<NT>Client secret</NT>**.
1. Open the **<NT>Workspace settings</NT>** page on <NT>Bitrise</NT> and select **<NT>Integrations</NT>**.
1. Select **<NT>Bitbucket Server</NT>** and then click **<NT>Add credentials</NT>**.

   ![2025-11-13-bitbucket-server-integration.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-6657cf32-779e-915b-aaea-453f88b28ec8.png)
1. Add your credentials:

   - **<NT>Client ID</NT>**
   - **<NT>Client secret</NT>**
   - **URL**: Your server's base URL
