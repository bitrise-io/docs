<NT><GlossTerm baseform="workspace">Workspace</GlossTerm></NT>s own projects. When you add a project to <NT>Bitrise</NT>, you select the <NT>workspace</NT> that will act as the owner of the project. From that point, only the owners of the <NT>workspace</NT> can change the ownership of the project.

It can happen that you need to transfer a project on [Bitrise](https://www.bitrise.io) to another <NT>workspace</NT>. This can be done in two ways:

- You can select the projects you want to transfer from the **<NT>Workspace settings</NT>** page's **Projects** menu.
- Any given project can be transferred from the project's **<NT>Project settings</NT>** page.

When transferring a project, its custom settings are also transferred. These include:

- All <NT><GlossTerm baseform="workflow">Workflow</GlossTerm></NT>s and <NT><GlossTerm baseform="pipeline">Pipeline</GlossTerm></NT>s.
- Build <NT><GlossTerm baseform="trigger">trigger</GlossTerm></NT>s.
- <NT>Environment Variables</NT>.
- <NT>Build artifacts</NT>.

Users on the project's team might lose their access in the event of a transfer.

- Users of the project who are owners of the <NT>workspace</NT> that receives the project will retain access.
- Contributors will be transferred and retain their access if the <NT>workspace</NT> that receives the project allows outside contributors. If the <NT>workspace</NT> doesn't allow outside contributors, they will lose their access.
- Users belonging to groups listed on the **<NT>Groups</NT>** tab will lose their access to the project, as the groups belong to the <NT>workspace</NT> that previously owned the project.


## Transferring projects on the <NT>Workspace settings</NT> page

:::important[Owners only]

You must be an owner of the <NT>Workspace</NT> to transfer its projects to another <NT>Workspace</NT>.

:::

1. <Partial_OpeningTheWorkspaceSettingsPage />
1. On the left, select **Projects** from the menu options.
1. Click the **<NT>Move project</NT> (↔)** button next to the project's name to select a project.

   :::note[Projects with outside contributors]

   When transferring projects with outside contributors to a <NT>Workspace</NT> that doesn't allow outside contributors, the existing contributors are automatically removed from the project's team.

   :::
1. Select a user or <NT>Workspace</NT> to transfer the project to them.

   ![move-project.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-5aa8c4c0-1851-b6c3-810b-11ca0c3dda28.png)
1. Click **<NT>Move project</NT>** to finalize.

   :::warning[Transferring ownership is permanent]

   Only the <NT>new owner</NT> can change the project's ownership after the transfer has been completed!

   :::


## Transferring a project on the <NT>Project settings</NT> page

1. <NT>Log in</NT> with an account that is an owner of the project.
1. Open the **<NT>Project settings</NT>** page.
1. Select **<NT>Basic settings</NT>** on the left and find the **Danger Zone** label.
1. Click the **<NT>Move project</NT>** button.

   ![move-project-button.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-2ce0bbbd-d455-1840-7b19-0bcfc6da8e12.png)
1. Select a <NT>Workspace</NT> to transfer the project to it.

   ![move-project-dialog.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-4c68fb66-ab5a-75a7-bb14-ab0f2617444f.png)
1. Click **<NT>Move project</NT>** to finalize.

   :::warning[Transferring ownership is permanent!]

   Only the <NT>new owner</NT> can change the project's ownership after the transfer has been completed!

   :::
