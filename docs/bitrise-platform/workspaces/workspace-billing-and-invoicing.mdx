You can modify your subscription plan and billing information at any time on the <NT><GlossTerm baseform="workspace">Workspace</GlossTerm></NT> page.

You can set up a credit card as payment method. We'll send you invoices to your email address but you can also download them from the **<NT>Workspace settings</NT>** page. Please note that direct debit is only available for <NT>Workspaces</NT> via our [Sales team](https://bitrise.io/contact).


## Changing your <NT>billing email</NT>

By default, a <NT>workspace</NT>’s invoices will be sent to the email provided when the <NT>workspace</NT> was created. However, the owner(s) of the <NT>workspace</NT> can change it at any time.

1. Sign in with an account that is an owner of the <NT>workspace</NT>.
1. <Partial_OpeningTheWorkspaceSettingsPage />
1. On the left, select **<NT>General settings</NT>** from the menu options.

   ![billing-email.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-d36425af-6967-473f-d8d7-07fdaed7ebc1.png)
1. Locate the **<NT>Billing email</NT>** field and click **<NT>Edit</NT>** under it.
1. Enter the new email address and click **<NT>Confirm changes</NT>**.


## Downloading <NT>Workspace</NT> invoices

1. Sign in with an account that is an owner of the <NT>Workspace</NT>.
1. <Partial_OpeningTheWorkspaceSettingsPage />
1. On the left, select **<NT>Plan & billing</NT>** from the menu options.
1. Click **<NT>Manage subscription</NT>**.
1. In the dialog, select **<NT>Billing</NT> <NT>History</NT>**.

   ![download-invoice.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-95fcc51e-94c6-ec28-5d06-5578439cf173.png)
1. Select the invoice you need and click **<NT>Download</NT>**.


## Changing a subscription plan

You can change your <NT>workspace</NT>'s subscription plan at any time from the **<NT>Workspace settings</NT>** page.

1. <Partial_OpeningTheWorkspaceSettingsPage />
1. On the left navigation menu, select **<NT>Plan & Billing</NT>**.
1. Click **<NT>Change plan</NT>**.
1. Select a <NT>new plan</NT> or change the tier of your existing plan.

   :::note[Enterprise plans]

   Enterprise plans are not available on a self-serve basis. If you wish to select an Enterprise plan, you can click the **<NT>Talk to us</NT>** button which takes you to our [contact page](https://bitrise.io/contact).

   :::

   ![plan-select.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-e09272e9-48fe-2dba-c52c-24b79391c293.png)
1. On the **<NT>Customize plan</NT>** page, <NT>set up</NT> your subscription plan and then click **<NT>Continue to checkout</NT>**.
1. Follow the instructions in the checkout dialog.

   You can change your payment method, billing address, and all other subscription information at this stage.

   ![checkout.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-85ec7e12-6811-a14d-5388-d5502f87fc22.png)


## Canceling a product subscription

In <NT>Bitrise</NT>, each product has its own independent subscription that you can cancel separately. You can cancel <NT>Bitrise CI</NT>, <NT>Build Cache</NT>, <NT>Release Management</NT>, or any other product without affecting your other subscriptions.

You can also cancel an add-on subscription on its own. Canceling an add-on doesn't affect the product's subscription — it stays active.

When you cancel a product subscription, you keep access until the end of your <NT>current billing period</NT>. After that, the product reverts to its free plan where available.

1. <Partial_OpeningTheWorkspaceSettingsPage />
1. On the left navigation menu, select **<NT>Plan & Billing</NT>**.
1. Find the product you want to cancel and click **<NT>Manage</NT>** on its card.
1. In the product drawer, click **<NT>Cancel subscription</NT>**.
1. Select a cancellation reason and click **<NT>Confirm</NT> cancellation**.

Your subscription remains active until the end of the <NT>current billing period</NT>. A banner on the **<NT>Plan & Billing</NT>** page confirms the scheduled cancellation date.

:::note

To cancel all product subscriptions, repeat these steps for each product individually.

:::
