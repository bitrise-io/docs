A <NT><GlossTerm baseform="workspace">Workspace</GlossTerm></NT> is an environment that allows you to manage your <NT>Bitrise</NT> apps and the team members working on the apps. You can create multiple <NT>Workspaces</NT>, and you can be invited to <NT>Workspaces</NT> by other <NT>Bitrise</NT> users.

You must have access to at least one <NT>Workspace</NT> to be able to use <NT>Bitrise</NT>. When you create a new account, you can’t add new apps or run builds. To be able to do so, you need to either create a <NT>Workspace</NT>, or to be invited to an existing one.

When you sign up for <NT>Bitrise</NT>, you are automatically prompted to create a new <NT>Workspace</NT>. If you skip creating a <NT>Workspace</NT>, or you want to create additional <NT>Workspaces</NT> later, you can do it:

1. <NT>Log in</NT> to [bitrise.io](https://www.bitrise.io).
1. Hover over the left navigation bar.
1. Open the dropdown menu next to your <NT>Workspace</NT> name.

   ![workspace-menu.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-c9c66545-e909-2236-e073-d55e134d6548.png)
1. Select **<NT>Create Workspace</NT>**. This takes you to the **<NT>Create</NT> a <NT>new Workspace</NT>** page.
1. Find the **<NT>Workspace name</NT>** input field, and add a name.

   ![create-workspace.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-09615e71-c7e5-0a43-b41d-b611e25b0c29.png)
1. Click **<NT>Create</NT>**.

   This creates the <NT>Workspace</NT> - everything that comes after is optional and can be completed at a later point. However, without a subscription plan - either free or paid - your <NT>Workspace</NT> will be inactive and won't be able to own apps or run builds.
1. Choose a plan.

   If you’re interested in the details, or you want to find out more about our other plans, check out our [Pricing page](https://www.bitrise.io/pricing/) for more info.
1. Select a paid subscription plan, and follow the instructions to configure the details of the plan and set up payment methods.

   Alternatively, select the free plan for now and [add a subscription later](/en/bitrise-platform/workspaces/workspace-billing-and-invoicing).

And that’s it. You can start adding apps and inviting people to your <NT>Workspace</NT>.
