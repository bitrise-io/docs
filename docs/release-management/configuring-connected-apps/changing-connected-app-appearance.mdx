All apps in <NT>Release Management</NT> have a title and app icon. You can edit the title and use your own custom icon.

:::important[<NT>App</NT> icon requirements]

The supported formats for the app icon are JPEG and PNG. It must have a non-transparent background. The maximum file size is 1 MB.

:::

1. <Partial_OpeningAnAppInReleaseManagement />
1. On the left, select **<NT>App settings</NT>**. Note that only **<NT>Project Admin</NT>s** and **<NT><GlossTerm baseform="workspace">Workspace</GlossTerm></NT> Owners** can access the **<NT>App settings</NT>** menu for an application.
1. In the **<NT>Appearance</NT>** section, you can:

   - Click **<NT>Edit title</NT>** to change the title. Set the new title in the dialog and then click **<NT>Save</NT>**.

     ![2025-08-08-rm-add-new-app-title.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-a8e8da8b-6759-e159-5aa4-6a97f83896dc.png)
   - Click **<NT>Change icon</NT>** to change the app icon. In the dialog, drag-and-drop a new icon. It must be a JPEG or a PNG with a non-transparent background, no bigger than 1 MB.

     ![2025-08-08-rm-change-app-icon.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-80fbbf58-d67b-34af-96aa-4b5124ef844e.png)

:::note[App icons and our API]

If you prefer, you can also change the app icon using our [API](https://api.bitrise.io/release-management/api-docs/index.html#/Connected%20Apps%20-%20Public%20Assets/GeneratePublicAssetUploadUrl).

:::
