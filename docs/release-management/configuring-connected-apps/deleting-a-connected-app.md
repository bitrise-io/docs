When you delete a connected app from Release Management, all data related to this app will be removed from Bitrise permanently.

To delete a connected app:

1. Log in to Bitrise, and from the left sidebar, select **Releases**.
1. From the **Connected apps** list, select your app.

   ![2025-08-07-rm-your-apps-list.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-c446a6a9-f922-2641-53ad-49099a7921fe.png)
1. On the left, select **App settings**.
1. On the bottom of the **Connected app settings** page, click **Delete app**. This opens the **Delete connected app** dialog.

   ![dialog-delete-app.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-c93bbd2a-6d87-97d3-11ee-ca32475064af.png)
1. Follow the instructions in the dialog to delete the app permanently.
