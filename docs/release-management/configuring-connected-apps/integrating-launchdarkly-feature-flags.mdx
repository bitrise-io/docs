A feature flag in [LaunchDarkly](https://docs.launchdarkly.com/home) describes the different variations of a feature and the rules that allow different entities to access them. You can follow your application's feature flags through the integration between LaunchDarkly and <NT>Bitrise</NT>. To do so, you'll need to:

1. [Connect your LaunchDarkly account to Bitrise.](#connecting-your-launchdarkly-account-to-bitrise)
1. [Select a LaunchDarkly project and environment for the connected app in Release management](#selecting-your-launchdarkly-project-and-environment).

## Connecting your LaunchDarkly account to <NT>Bitrise</NT>

1. Make sure you are logged in to LaunchDarkly.
1. Open <NT>Bitrise</NT>.
1. <Partial_GettingToYourProfileSettings />
1. On the left, select **<NT>Feature flags</NT>**.
1. In the **<NT>LaunchDarkly</NT>** section, click the **<NT>Connect</NT>** button.

   ![connect-launchdarkly.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-56915efe-d16d-4ab6-6535-f2b18d6ee6af.png)
1. You will be redirected to LaunchDarkly. Click **<NT>Authorize</NT>**.

You now should have access to your LaunchDarkly project data in <NT>Release Management</NT>.

## Selecting your LaunchDarkly project and environment

After you’ve [connected your LaunchDarkly account to Bitrise](#connecting-your-launchdarkly-account-to-bitrise), you will have access to your LaunchDarkly projects in <NT>Release Management</NT>.

To select a LaunchDarkly project and environment for your connected app:

1. <NT>Log in</NT> to <NT>Bitrise</NT>, and from the left sidebar, select **<NT>Releases</NT>**.
1. From the **<NT>Connected apps</NT>** list, select <NT>your app</NT>.

   ![2025-08-07-rm-your-apps-list.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-c446a6a9-f922-2641-53ad-49099a7921fe.png)
1. On the left sidebar, select **<NT>Feature flags</NT>**.
1. Click **<NT>Change</NT>** in the **<NT>Project and environment</NT>** section. It opens a dialog where you can edit the LaunchDarkly configuration for this connected app.

   ![launchdarkly.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-9d7fd7fc-e125-4f45-d011-f5ddcabe0106.png)
1. In the dialog window, select the <NT>LaunchDarkly</NT> project you wish to connect from the list and select the <NT>LaunchDarkly</NT> project environment you wish to use.
1. Select a LaunchDarkly project and environment.

   ![existing-launchdarkly-config.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-7902ae23-26f7-bcf3-ffee-11dd4e08a183.png)
1. Click **<NT>Save changes</NT>**.

Once done, you should be able to see the feature flags of the LaunchDarkly project in each release of the app.
