When you delete a connected app from <NT>Release Management</NT>, all data related to this app will be removed from <NT>Bitrise</NT> permanently.

To delete a connected app:

1. <NT>Log in</NT> to <NT>Bitrise</NT>, and from the left sidebar, select **<NT>Releases</NT>**.
1. From the **<NT>Connected apps</NT>** list, select <NT>your app</NT>.

   ![2025-08-07-rm-your-apps-list.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-c446a6a9-f922-2641-53ad-49099a7921fe.png)
1. On the left, select **<NT>App settings</NT>**.
1. On the bottom of the **<NT>Connected app</NT> settings** page, click **<NT>Delete app</NT>**. This opens the **<NT>Delete</NT> <NT>connected app</NT>** dialog.

   ![dialog-delete-app.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-c93bbd2a-6d87-97d3-11ee-ca32475064af.png)
1. Follow the instructions in the dialog to delete the app permanently.
