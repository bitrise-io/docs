:::warning[Deleting a release is permanent!]

Deleting a release is irreversible and you will lose all data of the release in <NT>Release Management</NT>.

Your data in TestFlight, <NT>App Store Connect</NT>, or <NT>Google Play</NT> will not be lost, and ongoing processes in the App Store or the <NT>Google Play Store</NT> will not be affected. App Store review will not be canceled. App Store phased releases will be automatically finished. <NT>Google Play</NT> staged rollouts will be stopped but you can finish the process manually on <NT>Google Play</NT>.

:::

To delete a release:

1. <Partial_GettingToReleaseConfiguration />
1. Scroll down to the **<NT>Delete release</NT>** section, and click **<NT>Delete release</NT>**.
1. In the dialog, click **<NT>Delete</NT>** to confirm.
