You can convert your old, existing <NT>Pipelines</NT> that contain stages into graph <NT>Pipelines</NT> without stages. This unlocks the full benefits of using <NT>Pipelines</NT>: faster build times, more granular configuration, and a full editing capability in the <NT>Workflow Editor</NT>.

<NT>Pipelines</NT> with stages are read-only in the <NT>Workflow Editor</NT>. To edit them, you need to convert them first. The original <NT>Pipeline</NT> won't be removed and will still work after the conversion, so your builds won't break. You can switch to using the new <NT>Pipeline</NT> when all your configurations are ready.

:::note

All new <NT>Pipeline</NT> features and improvements will only be available for graph <NT>Pipelines</NT>. <NT>Pipelines</NT> with stages will have no further features added.

:::

To convert a <NT>Pipeline</NT>:

1. Open the <NT>Workflow Editor</NT> and select the <NT>Pipeline</NT> you want to convert.
1. Click **Convert <NT>Pipeline</NT>** in the banner at the top of the canvas.

   ![Convert Pipeline banner](/img/workflows-and-pipelines/2026-07-06-convert-pipeline-banner.png)

   <NT>Bitrise</NT> creates a converted copy named `<original-id>_converted` and selects it automatically.

1. Review and adjust the converted <NT>Pipeline</NT> as needed.

   :::important[No cycles allowed]

   If your <NT>Pipeline</NT> conversion creates a cycle in the graph, you won't be able to save the <NT>Pipeline</NT>. You have to manually edit the configuration to remove the cycle.

   :::

1. Click **<NT>Save changes</NT>** in the top right corner.
