Step inputs are the way to configure the Steps for your build. Steps have required inputs that must have a valid value and optional inputs that provide more options to customize your build.

Click on a Step to bring up its input variables on the right of the currently selected <GlossTerm baseform="Workflow">Workflow</GlossTerm>. Required inputs are marked as such in the Workflow Editor. If required inputs do not have valid values, the Step will fail.

![Adding_step_input.png](/img/_paligo/uuid-c09d6420-4a98-85f8-8bdc-c96e59c07779.png)

Modify a Step input by either:

- Clicking into the input field.
- Clicking the **CHANGE** option next to the input field.

You can also use [Environment Variables (Env Vars) as Step inputs.](/en/bitrise-ci/configure-builds/environment-variables/using-an-env-var-as-a-step-input)
