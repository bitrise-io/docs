<GlossTerm baseform="step">Step</GlossTerm> inputs are the way to configure the Steps for your build. Steps have required inputs that must have a valid value and optional inputs that provide more options to customize your build.

Click on a Step to bring up its input variables on the right of the currently selected <GlossTerm baseform="Workflow">Workflow</GlossTerm>. Required inputs are marked as such in the <GlossTerm baseform="workflow editor">Workflow Editor</GlossTerm>. If required inputs do not have valid values, the Step will fail.

![Step input fields with Insert variable button](/img/workflows-and-pipelines/2026-07-06-step-input-insert-variable.png)

Modify a Step input by either:

- Clicking into the input field.
- Clicking the **Insert variable** button next to the input field to insert an Environment Variable as the value.

You can also use [Environment Variables (Env Vars) as Step inputs.](/en/bitrise-ci/configure-builds/environment-variables#using-an-env-var-in-a-step-input)
