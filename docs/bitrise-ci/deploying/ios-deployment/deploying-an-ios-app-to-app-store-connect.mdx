You can deploy an app to <NT>App Store Connect</NT> to:

- Invite testers on Testflight.
- Release your app on the App Store.

On <NT>Bitrise</NT>, you can either simply just upload your binary to <NT>App Store Connect</NT> or you can also submit it for review.


## <NT>Deploy to App Store Connect</NT> <NT>Steps</NT>

To deploy the app to <NT>App Store Connect</NT>, we have two <NT>Steps</NT>:

- **<NT>Deploy to App Store Connect</NT>**
- **<NT>Deploy to App Store Connect</NT> - Application Loader**

**<NT>Deploy to App Store Connect</NT> - Application Loader** is simple: it simply pushes an .ipa or .pkg binary file to <NT>App Store Connect</NT>. With this <NT><GlossTerm baseform="Step">Step</GlossTerm></NT>, you cannot submit the app for review on the App Store, for example.

With the **<NT>Deploy to App Store Connect</NT>** <NT>Step</NT>, you can:

- Submit your app to the App Store for review.
- You can upload apps of three different platforms (iOS, OS X, AppleTVOS).
- Tell <NT>Bitrise</NT> whether you want to upload your screenshots and the app’s metadata along with the binary.
